Our Story

A Taste of Prague,
Brewed with Love

Praha Cafe was born from a simple longing — the memory of slow mornings in Prague's historic kavárnas, where coffee was more than a drink; it was a conversation, a ritual, a moment of stillness in a beautiful city.

Founded in 2021 by Czech expat Tomáš Dvořák, our cafe brings the warmth of Bohemian coffee culture to life. Every cup is roasted with beans sourced from small farms, prepared with methods passed down through generations of Prague's finest coffee houses.

Step inside and you'll find exposed brick walls, the scent of freshly baked trdelník, brass fixtures that catch the morning light, and a quietness that invites you to stay a little longer.
